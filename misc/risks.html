Risks

Vulnerability

As ransomware attacks can occur from the simple act of opening an email attachment or link, It can have a great impact on companies or users who have no protection or methods to protect against such attacks. (Reeve, 2021). This vulnerability causes problems when dealing with a critical industry such as healthcare where without access to specific files or systems, downtime can cause someone their life as every second matters. This is a high-risk factor as there is very little downtime to patch or find a resolution to these types of attacks.

Financial risks

According to a research, in 2021, 55% of New Zealand businesses had been a subject to a ransomware attack. Recent ransomware attack in Australia was targeting the aged care. It was crucial because aged people tend to need more surgical operations and they have more medical issues than the younger ones (Australian Government, 2021). The article also states that paying the money does not guarantee the access to the data. It was predicted that the financial damage cost ranged from 50 billion dollars in 2017 to 200 billion dollars in less than 6 years.
